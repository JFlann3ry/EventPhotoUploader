About {{ WEBSITE_NAME }}

{{ WEBSITE_NAME }} is designed to make sharing and collecting event memories simple and secure. Whether you're hosting a wedding, party, conference, or any special occasion, our platform allows your guests to easily upload their photos and videos, so everyone can relive the best moments together.

How It Works

  • Organizers create an event and share a unique code or QR link with guests.
  • Guests use the code or link to access the upload page and submit their photos.
  • All uploads are collected in a private gallery for the event.

Why Choose Us?

  • Easy for everyone: No app required, works on any device.
  • Private & Secure: Only people with the event code and password can upload or view photos.
  • Organised Memories: All your event photos in one place, ready to download or share.
