How It Works

  1. Get Your Event Code:
    The event organizer will provide you with an event code and password, or a QR code to scan.
  2. Access the Upload Page:
    Visit the website and click "Guest Login" or scan the QR code to go directly to the upload page for your event.
  3. Enter Your Details:
    Enter your email address and the event code/password if prompted.
  4. Select Your Photos:
    Choose the photos (and videos, if allowed) you want to share from your device.
  5. Upload:
    Click "Upload" to send your files securely to the event gallery.
  6. View & Download:
    Once approved by the organizer, you and other guests can view and download photos from the event gallery.
